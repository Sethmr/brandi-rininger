About Brevard, NC

Brevard is known worldwide as the "Land of Waterfalls" and serves as the gateway to some of Western North Carolina's most stunning natural wonders. Nestled in Transylvania County, this charming mountain town combines outdoor adventure with vibrant cultural amenities and a thriving creative community. With a population of approximately 7,700, Brevard maintains a small-town charm while offering excellent schools, diverse dining, and year-round activities.

The area is particularly renowned for its exceptional natural beauty. Over 250 waterfalls cascade through Transylvania County, including White Water Falls—the highest waterfall in the Eastern United States at 411 feet—and the iconic Looking Glass Falls at 60 feet. Pisgah National Forest provides endless opportunities for hiking, biking, climbing, and paddling, while nearby DuPont State Forest offers additional trails and natural water features that draw outdoor enthusiasts from across the country.

Beyond outdoor recreation, Brevard boasts a distinctive character marked by its creative spirit and cultural attractions. The town is famous for its white squirrels, which have become beloved local symbols. The Brevard Music Center hosts summer performances that attract musicians and music lovers worldwide, while local galleries, breweries, and Brevard College contribute to a vibrant arts scene. The downtown area features independent shops, galleries, pubs, and free parking, creating a welcoming destination for both residents and visitors.

Neighborhoods in Brevard

Downtown Brevard

The heart of Brevard features walkable streets lined with independent shops, galleries, and pubs. Free parking and easy pedestrian access make this area ideal for those seeking a vibrant community atmosphere with shopping and dining options at hand.

Pisgah Forest

This neighborhood serves as the gateway to Pisgah National Forest and the scenic Davidson River. Perfect for outdoor enthusiasts who want direct access to hiking trails, fishing, and adventure opportunities while maintaining community amenities.

Rosman

A quieter, more rural area offering affordable mountain living with a peaceful atmosphere. Rosman appeals to those seeking a slower pace while still benefiting from proximity to Brevard's amenities and outdoor recreation.

Cedar Mountain

A higher-elevation mountain community offering spectacular views and a more secluded setting. Cedar Mountain attracts buyers seeking privacy and natural beauty with a true mountain living experience.

Lifestyle & Amenities

Outdoor Recreation

Experience 250+ waterfalls, Pisgah National Forest, DuPont State Forest, hiking, biking, rock climbing, paddling, and waterfall chasing. The natural water slide, Davidson River, and countless trails offer endless outdoor adventures for all skill levels.

Cultural Attractions

Brevard Music Center hosts summer performances featuring world-class musicians. Local galleries showcase regional and visiting artists. Breweries, Brevard College cultural events, and the creative community foster a vibrant arts scene year-round.

Dining & Culinary

Over 12 local restaurants, cafes, and dessert shops serve diverse cuisines. Brevard has become a foodie destination, with establishments emphasizing local ingredients, farm-to-table practices, and creative culinary experiences that reflect mountain culture.

Community Character

Known worldwide for white squirrels and outdoor culture, Brevard exudes creative spirit. The town fosters a welcoming, inclusive atmosphere where outdoor enthusiasts, artists, musicians, and nature lovers come together to celebrate mountain living.

Schools in Brevard Area

Brevard is served by the Transylvania County Schools system, which maintains above-average educational ratings. The school system includes:

  • Brevard High School - Comprehensive secondary education serving the Brevard area
  • Rosman High School - Secondary school serving the Rosman community
  • Brevard Middle School - Middle grades education with strong academics and programs
  • Pisgah Forest Elementary - Elementary education serving the Pisgah Forest area
  • Brevard Elementary - Primary education for Brevard students

The schools maintain above-average ratings and are complemented by Brevard College, which contributes to the area's emphasis on education and cultural enrichment. The proximity to quality schools makes Brevard an attractive option for families seeking excellent education in a mountain setting.
