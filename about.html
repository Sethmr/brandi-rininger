About Brandi

Brandi Rininger is a licensed real estate broker with eXp Realty and the Rookie of the Year in Western North Carolina, proudly serving homebuyers, sellers, and land investors across the Carolina Smokies region. From the charming streets of Franklin and Bryson City to the vibrant culture of Asheville, Brandi helps clients find their perfect place in the mountains.

Brandi and her family chose to put down roots in Western North Carolina because they love the mountains, the tight-knit communities, and the quality of life that comes with living here. That same passion drives her work every day — she treats every client like family, listens first, advises honestly, and is always just a call or text away, even after closing.

Whether you're buying your first home, selling a family property, or searching for land in the Blue Ridge, Brandi brings a personal touch that's rare in this industry. She knows the neighborhoods, the market trends, and the hidden gems most agents overlook — from Franklin to Asheville, Highlands to Bryson City, and everywhere in between.
